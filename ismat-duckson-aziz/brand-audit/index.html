Long-Term Authority

Wikipedia Notability Path

Wikipedia requires verifiable coverage in independent, reliable sources with editorial standards — not press releases or self-authored content. This is the staged approach to earning it properly.

Current Status: Ismat does not yet qualify for a Wikipedia article. Her existing press (Reuters recognition, Homethrive PR Newswire, Kemper investor release) is a mix of awards, press releases, and announcement wire copy — these do not satisfy Wikipedia's "significant coverage in independent, reliable sources" standard. Moving too early risks a deletion nomination, which creates a negative signal. The PR campaign and editorial placement strategy in this plan are the direct prerequisites.

01
Existing Coverage Audit
Document all existing press mentions. Assess which qualify as independent editorial sources vs. wire copy or self-authored releases. Reuters recognition qualifies. PR Newswire does not.
Complete
02
Editorial Placement Drive
Secure 3–5 independent editorial features or bylined articles in publications with editorial standards: Forbes, HBR, Fast Company, HR Executive, SHRM publications. Each is a citable Wikipedia source.
In Progress
03
Notability Check
Formal review against Wikipedia's notability guidelines for people (WP:PERSON). Minimum threshold: significant coverage in multiple independent, reliable sources. Conducted once editorial placement drive is complete.
Upcoming
04
Article Draft + Submission
Neutral, factual, third-person article drafted by a Wikipedia-experienced writer. Sourced exclusively from independent media. Submitted via Articles for Creation process once notability criteria are confirmed met.
